MICHAEL MIDDLETON

I’m an engineer from the UK, living in the US.
My background is in aeronautical engineering, focusing on
control theory, vehicle dynamics modeling & simulation, and V&V.
I spend my working hours developing related tools and solutions for aerospace and marine systems projects.
​
I have a deep appreciation for graphic design and data visualization, pursuing these as a hobby and occasionally for commissioned work. I enjoy making infographics, maps and diagrams to convey information with accuracy, coherence, and visual appeal. Exploring the non-linear relationship of what looks good versus what is most intuitive is exciting and I love breaking convention to express real data in interesting ways.
​
I also make the occasional digital art doodle and very amateur vexillography (flag design), and have coded up several interactive projects that you can potter about with. This site is a something of a casual portfolio for all of the above, often with some connection to aerospace, submarines, flags and cartography. Take a browse through my projects using the navigation buttons on the left. Enjoy!
