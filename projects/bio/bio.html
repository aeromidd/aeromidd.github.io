MICHAEL MIDDLETON

I’m an engineer from the UK. My background is in aeronautical engineering, with a specific focus on control theory, numerical modelling & simulation; I spend my working hours developing related tools and solutions for aerospace and marine systems projects, with experience in both civil and defense sectors.
​
I have a keen interest in graphic design and data visualization, pursuing these as a hobby and also occasionally for commissioned work. I enjoy making infographics, maps and diagrams to convey information with accuracy, coherence, and visual appeal.
​
I also do the occasional comic doodle and very amateur vexillography (flag design), and have coded up several interactive projects that you can potter about with.
​
This site is a something of a casual portfolio for all of the above. Take a browse through my projects using the navigation buttons on the left. Enjoy!
